My Story

I'm Hisyam Abilkhoir, a full-stack developer with a relentless passion for creating impactful digital solutions. Over the past 6+ years, I've had the privilege of working with startups, agencies, and enterprise clients across diverse industries — from e-commerce and fintech to health tech and real estate.

My journey began with building simple websites, but quickly evolved into a deep fascination with the entire software development ecosystem. Today, I architect complete systems — from user-facing interfaces built with React and Next.js, to robust backend APIs powered by Node.js and Laravel, all deployed on scalable cloud infrastructure.

What sets me apart is not just technical skill, but a genuine commitment to understanding the business problem behind every project. I believe that the best software is built when technology serves strategy — and that's the approach I bring to every engagement.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or mentoring aspiring developers. I believe in continuous learning and giving back to the community that helped shape my career.
